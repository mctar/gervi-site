About

Current Focus

Gervi Labs is an independent research lab focused on how people and synthetic agents interact, create, and share control. We build agents, sensors, and tools that let groups experience new forms of interaction with synthetic systems.

Some prototypes become performances. Others become internal protocols or research material. Each experiment helps us understand how agency shifts when humans and machines work together — and leaves a digital fossil for future study.

We work globally from Norway with festivals, conferences, institutions, companies, and research groups.
