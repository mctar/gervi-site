**Not a Cookbook** is among the first cookbooks composed entirely with AI systems. No human chefs were consulted for the recipes; synthetic collaborators handled every ingredient pairing, instruction, and illustration. Thordur Arnason co-wrote and edited the project to probe how generative tools reshape creative production under a sixteen-week constraint.

### Why we made it

The experiment investigates how generative AI can augment human capability rather than replace it. Working through a rapid toolchain highlighted both the limitations and expanding potential of emerging models. The process aligns with the Engelbart-era vision of human augmentation instead of automation anxiety.

### Toolchain

- **GPT-3 Davinci-002 / GPT-3.5 Davinci-003 (KIM):** narrative voice and recipe generation.
- **FlavourGraph & FlavourDB (Flaivia):** molecular-level ingredient and flavor pairings.
- **Midjourney, DALLÂ·E 2, Stable Diffusion:** artwork, images, and speculative photography.
- **Resemble.ai (AIson):** voice clone for audiobook narration tests.
- Additional research agents and utilities contributed, even if their outputs did not ship in the final publication.
